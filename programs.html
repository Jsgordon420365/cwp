Charlotte Programs

In Charlotte, we conduct sessions at the Evolution Aquatic Center in Huntersville. Practices are held on Sunday afternoons from 4:00 to 5:30 PM, catering to boys and girls aged 10 to 18. No prior experience is necessary, but participants should be confident in deep water.

Holiday Clinics

Upcoming clinics are scheduled for December 28 at the Mecklenburg County Aquatic Center (MCAC) and December 29 at the Evolution Aquatic Center (EAC).
