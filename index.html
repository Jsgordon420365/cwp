About Us

Carolina Water Polo Club offers youth water polo programs across North Carolina, including in the Charlotte area. We provide seasonal and year-round training for athletes of all ages and skill levels.

Join Us

We encourage new athletes to join at any time, offering pro-rated fees and custom programs. Free trial sessions are available to help newcomers get acquainted with the sport.
